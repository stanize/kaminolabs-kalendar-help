**Business** is where you define how clients see you when they book with you.

## Business details

- **Business name** — shown on your booking page and in the emails your clients receive.
- **Business type** — choose from psychology, nutrition, physiotherapy, beauty & aesthetics, fitness & training, coaching, classes & tutoring, or other. This helps us tailor the service templates we suggest later.
- **City** — optional, shown alongside your business name.

## Your booking page link

When you create your business, you choose a unique link, e.g. `wellness-center`. That link becomes your public booking page.

**Important: the link is permanent.** Once created, you won't be able to change it yourself; if you need to change it later, you'll have to contact support. Choose carefully before confirming.

As you type, we check whether the link is available in real time. If it's already taken, we'll let you know so you can try another variation.

### Link review

Every link goes through an automated check before going fully live:

- If your link looks fine, your page goes **active** immediately and enters a background review queue.
- If the link might not be appropriate, it stays **under review** until confirmed by our team.
- In some cases a link can be **rejected**, and you'll need to choose another by contacting support.

You'll see your link's status (active, under review, or rejected) right on this page.

## Editing your business

You can come back to this page anytime to update the name, type, or city. As explained above, the link can't be edited from here once created.
