**Calendar** is where you see every booking coming in from your public page, grouped by day (Today, Tomorrow, and following dates).

## The two tabs

- **Upcoming** — all your confirmed appointments that are coming up.
- **Pending** — bookings made by clients who don't have an account yet (guest bookings) and are waiting for your confirmation. You'll see a counter with the number of pending bookings.

## Confirmed vs. pending bookings

When a client books while signed in with a client account, the appointment is **confirmed** instantly. When they book as a guest without creating an account, the appointment stays **pending confirmation** for 24 hours: you review it and decide whether to confirm it.

Pending bookings show how much time is left before they expire. If they aren't confirmed in time, they expire automatically.

## Available actions

- **Confirm** a pending booking to accept it for good.
- **Cancel** any booking, confirmed or pending. Cancelling automatically notifies the client by email.

## Automatic notifications

Every time a new booking comes in, you get an email alert. Your clients also get an email confirming their appointments, and an email if you cancel one.
