If you run into a problem or a question this guide doesn't answer, you can write to us directly from your dashboard.

## Sending a request

Go to **Support** in your dashboard sidebar and fill out the form:

- **Category** — choose from technical issue, billing or payments, feature suggestion, your account, or other.
- **Subject** — a short summary of your question.
- **Description** — tell us in detail what happened or what you need.
- **Screenshots** (optional) — you can attach up to 5 images, up to 5 MB each, which is useful for showing us exactly what you're seeing.

Once submitted, you'll see an on-screen confirmation. Our team will review your request and get back to you.

## Changing your booking page link

Since your page link is permanent and can't be edited from the dashboard (see **Your business**), changing it is exactly the kind of thing to reach out to support about.

## Before you write in

Take a look through the other guides in this help center — Getting started, Your business, Services, Team, Availability, Calendar, Clients, and Your booking page — in case your question is already answered there. If you can't find the answer, feel free to reach out.
