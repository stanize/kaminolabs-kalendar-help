Kalendar is the online booking platform for businesses like yours: psychology, nutrition, physiotherapy, beauty, fitness, coaching, or tutoring. This guide walks you through getting started.

## Creating your account

Sign up with your email and password, or with your Google account. If you sign up with email, we'll send you a confirmation link — open it from your inbox to activate your account. With Google, your account is verified instantly.

While you confirm your email, you can already enter your dashboard, but you'll see a screen asking you to confirm your account before continuing.

## Your dashboard

The first time you log in you'll land on **Home**, where you'll see a checklist to get your booking page ready:

1. **Set up your business** — name, business type, and city.
2. **Create your services** — what your clients will be able to book.
3. **Add your team** — just you, or several people.
4. **Set your availability** — the days and hours you accept appointments.

You can complete these steps in any order. Once all four are done, you'll see "All set!" and your booking page will be live for clients to book online.

## No rush

You don't need to finish everything at once. You can save each step and come back later — your progress is saved. The sidebar lets you move between Business, Services, Team, Availability, Calendar, and Support at any time.
