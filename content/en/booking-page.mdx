Your booking page is the public link you share with clients so they can book online, without needing to call or message you.

## Your link

It looks like `kalendar.kaminolabs.dev/bookings/your-link`, where `your-link` is the one you chose when setting up your business (see the **Your business** guide). You can share it via WhatsApp, social media, your website, or wherever works best.

## What clients see when booking

Booking happens in a few simple steps:

1. **Choose a service** — from the list you set up in Services.
2. **Choose a provider** — only if you work as a team; if you work solo, this step doesn't appear.
3. **Choose a date and time** — a weekly calendar shows available slots based on your configured availability.
4. **Identify themselves** — the client chooses between signing in with Google, signing in with an existing account, creating a new account, or continuing as a guest.
5. **Their details** (only if booking as a guest) — name, email, and optional phone number.
6. **Confirmation** — a final message that differs depending on the case: with an account, they see their booking is confirmed; as a guest, they see their request will be reviewed within 24 hours.

## Page language

Your booking page shows in Spanish by default, with a switcher so clients can change it to English if they prefer.

## Duplicate bookings

The system automatically prevents two people from booking the same slot at once: if someone else confirms that slot while your client is filling out the form, they're notified to pick another time.
