**Services** is where you define what shows up on your booking page for clients to choose from.

## Adding a service

Each service has three fields:

- **Name** — e.g. "First consultation".
- **Duration** — in minutes.
- **Price** — in euros. You can mark it as **Free** when the price is 0.

To add a new one, click **Add service** and fill in the form. Price is adjusted with a slider or by typing the amount directly into the box.

## Not sure where to start?

If you don't have any services yet, we show you a set of common templates based on your business type (for example, physiotherapy templates if that's your business type). You can:

1. Select several templates at once.
2. Customize each one before confirming (name, duration, and price).
3. Add them all to your service list together.

## Editing, reordering, and deleting

- Click **Edit** on any service to change its name, duration, or price.
- Drag services to change the order they appear in on your booking page.
- Click **Delete** to remove a service you no longer offer.

## How clients see it

On your public page, choosing one of these services is the very first step of booking. The order you set here is what clients will see.
